Pottery as a craft form is easily perhaps
among the most timeless living craft traditions of
India,practiced in the remotest corners across the
entire expanse of the country. Pottery belongs to a
Mesolithic ceramic tradition that developed among
Vindhya hunter-gatherers during the Mesolithic
period, while there are also debates saying that it
belonged to the Neolithic period. Pottery and
ceramic which were items of utility for
generations are important sources of livelihood
for scores of artisans across the country.
